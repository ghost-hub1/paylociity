Awards

We believe people matter most. Empowered, inspired employees serve clients best and drive more innovation, leading to greater impact for our clients and across the industry. Forward is our state of mind. Together is our action plan awards are icing on the cake.

Newsweek - America's Greatest Workplaces for Diversity 2024

America's Greatest Workplaces for Diversity 2024 award from Newsweek highlights Paylocitys commitment to fostering diversity, equity, inclusion, and accessibility (DEIA). This award recognizes Paylocitys culture as a diverse workforce and fundamental to cultivating creativity, innovation, and success.

CODiE Best HCM Technology

The SIIA CODiE Awards have honored thousands of software, education, information and media products, leaders and teams for achieving excellence. The rigorous evaluation means only the best solutions are honored, and Paylocity has won for Best HCM Technology.

Read More

Next-Gen HCM Tech NEAT Report

NelsonHalls Next Generation HCM Technology NEAT report ranks vendors based on their ability to provide immediate benefits to their clients, as well as a strong capability to meet their clients' future requirements. Paylocity is a ranked Leader for the Enterprise, as well as Small/Mid markets.
