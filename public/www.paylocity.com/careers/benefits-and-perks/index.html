Yes, weve got medical, dental, vision, life, disability and a 401(k) match. But what about all the other good stuff? As a Paylocity employee, you get the perks andbenefitsthat put you, your family and your finances first. Join us and youll enjoy:

  • Paid Parental Leave
  • Adoption Assistance
  • Milkstork program for moms who travel on business to ship breastmilk and get fully reimbursed

  • Health and wellness programs ranging from Naturally Slim to Ovia Health to Hinge Health
  • Healthy snack options like fresh fruit, hummus, and trail mixes
  • Discounted Employee Stock Purchasing program
  • Tuition Reimbursement
  • SoFi programto consolidate and refinance federal and private student loans
  • Volunteers In Action program for paid time off to volunteer
  • Onsite workout facilities and gym membership subsidies
  • Sparkling and flat flavored water, gourmet coffee drinks, hot chocolate

There are fun, colorful spaces throughout our offices to spark creativity. Take a call from a hanging swing chair, collaborate with your team in an open-air nook, or get focused in a quiet conference room. Scholarship Opportunities

Peter J. McGrail Scholarship

In honor of our late CFO, Peter J. McGrail, weve established a scholarship program to advance education among the next generation. In keeping with Peters passion for learning, this program helps offset the cost of college education. The scholarship is open to dependent children of employees who have completed at least two years of continuous service with Paylocity.

Peter J. McGrail:
A Transformative Leader

In a career spanning more than 25 years, Peter J. McGrail worked as a financial executive for highly successful public and privately held companies and served as Paylocitys Chief Financial Officer from 2010 through 2017. Peters passion for driving a learning and development culture across the organization guided Paylocitys transition to a publicly-traded company in 2014. He will always be remembered for his wisdom, quick wit, genuineness, and willingness to help others all hallmarks of Peters legacy. The Peter J. McGrail Learning Center at Paylocitys headquarters in Schaumburg, Illinois, is also dedicated in his memory.

A Scholarship for Future Changemakers

In honor of Peter, Paylocity established a scholarship program in 2017 to advance education for future generations. In keeping with Peters passion for learning, the award was created in his legacy by his Paylocity colleagues to assist children of employees who plan to continue their education, upholding Peters steadfast belief that learning and curiosity propel lives.We hope all recipients will use it to capitalize on their potential and the power of education to become transformational leader.
