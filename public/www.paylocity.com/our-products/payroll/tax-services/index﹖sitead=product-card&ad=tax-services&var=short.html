Tax Services Overview

Receive Timely, Reliable Tax Support

Verified federal returns are e-filed within 24 hours. We assume liability, penalty, or interest charge in the event of a Paylocity error.

Prepare for the Year Ahead

Visualize year-end tasks with quarterly and year-end insights dashboards.

Reconcile Funds Automatically

Experience confidential reporting with all funds reconciled for you by tax code. We also show company-level and employee-level tax setup in real time.

Stay Compliant

We resolve tax notices on your behalf, with no change to your address with taxing authorities, and provide visibility into federal and state deposits.

Ensure Accurate Processing

We implement check-editing capabilities during payroll processing to ensure your returns are error-free. We also automatically alert you of any potential data-validity issues.

Handle International Employees

Paylocity and Blue Marble provide integrated global solutions to help manage foreign currencies, time zones, and unique compliance requirements for all your payroll and tax filing needs.
